2022年元旦

The first morning of year 2022

第一缕阳光透过薄纱铺洒床头。
The sun shines sprinkled upper on my bed through the tulle.
微冷的空气中弥漫来阵阵花香。
The scent of flowers permeated in the cold air.
我张开怀抱,
I opened my arms,
迎接新年的来到。
Hugged the new year.
